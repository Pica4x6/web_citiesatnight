The rules

The three users that make the most successful classifications between 4 October at 00:00 GMT and 11 October 00:00 GMT will win (at least one of the Winners have to be a Woman).

What is a successful classification?

We will consider a classification to be successful when you can really identify the city and there are enough data to confirm it is the real target. The "I don't knows" will not be taken into account. Every 5 "I don't knows" will add one score to the final score. If two users have the same score, the one with fewere "I don't knows" will win.

What is the prize?

The price consist on a Revel Model of the ISS, a 76/300 dobson telescope and a LEGO Research institute.

The winners will choose according to their score.
